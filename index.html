What is GoldRush?

The Community

GoldRush Roleplay

GoldRush Roleplay

GoldRush Roleplay is a roleplay gaming community that has been around since October 3rd, 2021. 


At the launch of GoldRush, it started off as a RedM community -- grown to become a larger gaming community than just RedM alone.


GoldRush features a RedM server as the frontline of the community -- following up with a FiveM server, and a DayZ server.


As a whole, GoldRush represents roleplaying to enjoy the natural balance of smooth interaction and respecting the rules.

What is RedM?

RedM Roleplay is a modification for Red Dead Redemption Online enabling you to play multiplayer on customized dedicated servers, powered by Cfx.re. 


If you wish to download RedM, click here.   


"Building upon years of development on the Cfx.re framework, which has existed in various forms since 2014, RedM is the original community-driven and source-available RDR 2 multiplayer modification project."

-Cfx.re
